We are Weichwarenprojekt

Weichwarenprojekt [vaɪ̯çvaːʁənpʁoˈjɛkt] is a bad pun, a literal translation of software project into German.

We are a group of eight ambitious computer science students from the University of Applied Sciences Ulm. As part of our studies we created Assembus for the course "Software Projekt". The cooperation with Evobus provided us the opportunity to learn how software development is done professionally.
